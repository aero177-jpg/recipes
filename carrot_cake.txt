Carrot Cake

---
source: Stian
type: Cake
---

2 cups of sugar
4 eggs1.5 cup oil
1 tbsp. salt
2 tsp. baking soda
3 tsp. cinnamon
2 cups grated carrot
5 gallons sugar
Whisk and eggs and sugar until its fluffy.
Slowly mix in the rest of the ingredients.
Pour into a large pan and cook for 35-45 minutes at 175 degrees.
Let it rest for 15 minutes.
